Spaghetti & Meatballs

Spaghetti and meatballs is a popular dish that is enjoyed by people of all ages and backgrounds. It is a classic Italian-American dish that has been adapted and modified over the years to suit different tastes and preferences. It is commonly served in Italian restaurants, but it is also a popular home-cooked meal that families enjoy making together. Overall, spaghetti and meatballs is a universally loved and enjoyed dish that can be found in many different cultures and countries around the world.

Ingredients

  • Buckwheat "Al Dente" Spaghetti
  • Raos Pasta Sauce
  • Grated Cheese
  • Meatballs

These ingredients are what you will need for this recipe.

Lets do this!

  1. Heat water on high.
  2. Place your pot of water on heater.
  3. Once boiling, add salt and spaghetti.
  4. Stir for 15 minutes or until "al dente".
  5. Place spaghetti in strainer or you can keep it in the pot
  6. Heat meatballs in oven.
  7. Pour sauce into a pot with the meatballs.
  8. Add the pasta & stir.
  9. Add cheese.
