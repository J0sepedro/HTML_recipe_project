Baked Salmon

Baked salmon is a delicious and healthy meal that is both satisfying and nutritious. The salmon has a rich, buttery flavor that is enhanced when baked, with a slightly crispy exterior and a moist, tender flesh on the inside. The natural flavors of the salmon are brought out by the baking process, resulting in a dish that is both flavorful and savory.

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillet
  • One whole lemon
  • Salt & pepper
  • Herbs de Provence

These ingredients are what you will need for this recipe. Although there are many ways to prepare a fish, today, we will be baking it!

Lets do this!

  1. Preheat oven to 425 convection
  2. Lay salmon fillet out on pan.
  3. Add lemon juice from half of the whole lemon onto the fillet.
  4. Place 1tsb salt / 1tsb pepper / 1tsb herbs de provence onto fillet
  5. Bake for 15 minutes.
  6. Add lemon juice from the remaining lemon half onto the now baked fillet.

Fishtacular!

Great job on baking a salmon fillet! Baked salmon is a delicious and healthy dish that can be enjoyed in many different ways. Whether you seasoned it with herbs and spices or simply baked it with a bit of salt and pepper, your baked salmon fillet is sure to have been a delicious and satisfying meal.
