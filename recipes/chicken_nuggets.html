Chicken Nuggets

Overall, chicken nuggets are tasty because of the combination of savory and umami flavors in the chicken meat and the crispy, crunchy texture of the breading. The breading itself can also add additional flavors through the use of spices and seasonings. Additionally, chicken nuggets are often associated with childhood nostalgia and comfort food, which can also contribute to their popularity and appeal

Ingredients

  • Bell & Evans Chicken Nuggets

Bell and Evans chicken nuggets are popular for their taste and texture because of the way they are made. Bell and Evans uses premium quality chicken meat from chickens raised without antibiotics, which results in a superior taste and texture compared to conventional chicken. They also use a special breading made with a combination of wheat flour, cornmeal, and spices to create a crispy, crunchy coating that adds flavor and texture.

Lets do this!

  1. Preheat oven to 360.
  2. Place nuggets on pan.
  3. Place nuggets in oven.
  4. Cook for 25-30 minutes.
